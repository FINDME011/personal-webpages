About My self.

Hi there! As a fourth-year student of civil technology, I have a strong interest in construction and discovering the mysteries around the creation of buildings. You can find me riding difficult mountain bike trails or making baskets on the basketball court when I'm not engaged in textbooks. The peaceful surroundings that come next enhance the joy of the journey. Every building I pass throughout the day draws my attention with its intricate characteristics; they all have a distinct narrative to tell. Come have discussions with me that eloquently combine the domains of academics, outdoor enthusiasts, and building!

Passion

My enjoyment of a good cup of coffee and my enthusiasm for mountain riding go together beautifully with my respect of the natural world and my search for peaceful locations. Riding my bike through difficult trails is an exhilarating experience that takes me on a spiritual and physical journey that enables me to establish a connection with nature. My happiness is enhanced by the simple pleasure of sipping a nice cup of coffee while taking in the breathtaking scenery of nature as I tackle routes and discover new areas. Whether I'm in the peaceful haven of a forest or strolling along a mountainous trail, these encounters help to create a peaceful story. I find a deep sense of clarity in these serene settings with the comforting sounds of nature surrounding me.p>

Family

Having loving and responsible parents has laid the foundation for a happy and supportive family in our household. The nurturing care and guidance provided by my father and mother create an environment where each family member feels valued and cherished. Their commitment to responsibility sets a positive example, fostering a sense of stability and security. In this atmosphere of love and support, we navigate life's challenges together, creating a strong family bond that forms the backbone of our shared happiness.

Journey

As I journeyed through life, I embraced a friendly and joyful spirit, cultivating numerous friendships along the way. My childhood exuded happiness, and as I matured, I discovered a passion for mountain biking, relishing the thrill and excitement it brought to my days. Now, as I navigate adulthood and pursue my chosen career, I reflect with gratitude on the wealth of experiences that have shaped me. From the exhilarating rides to the fulfillment of my soul during OJT days spent in diverse locations, each adventure has etched indelible memories, contributing to the person I am today. The varied landscapes of both my personal and professional pursuits have enriched my life's journey, creating a mosaic of joy, friendship, and meaningful experiences.

Future Plan

In the pursuit of my future plan, I aspire to secure a fulfilling job that not only caters to my essential needs but also allows me the means to fulfill my desires. The vision extends beyond borders, as I aim to venture abroad to seek new opportunities and experiences. Embracing the challenges and diversity of life in another country, I anticipate broadening my horizons, both personally and professionally. With a commitment to hard work and continuous growth, I look forward to a future where my endeavors lead to a prosperous and satisfying life overseas.
